Brain Train
Institute

The Brain Train Institute in Kapan, Kathmandu, offers a wide range of academic services from primary school (Form 1) to advanced levels, including computer and bridge courses. They also conduct motivational classes, fostering personal and academic growth in a family-like atmosphere. With a focus on holistic development and a supportive community environment, the institute aims to inspire and empower students of all ages and backgrounds to excel in their educational journey.

Rules and Regulations


Respect for Others: Students must treat peers, teachers, and staff with kindness, respect, and empathy at all times. Care for Materials: Students are responsible for handling Montessori materials with care, returning them to their designated places after use, and keeping the learning environment clean and orderly.

Participation: Students are encouraged to actively engage in classroom activities, follow instructions from teachers, and contribute positively to group discussions and collaborative projects.

Safety First: Students must adhere to safety guidelines and rules to ensure their own well-being and that of their classmates, refraining from behaviors that could cause harm or disruption.

Attendance and Punctuality: Regular attendance and punctuality are essential for effective learning and classroom dynamics. Students should arrive on time and attend classes regularly, with parents notifying the school in case of absences.
